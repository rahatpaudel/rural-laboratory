Step 1

Take an empty plastic bottle.

Step 2

Use the circular lid of the bottle to cut out a convex piece from the round part of the bottle.

Step 3

Pour some water into the plastic piece. The water will act as a magnifying glass.

How it works

The water behaves as convex lens. It is a type of lens which is thicker at the center and thinner at the edges. A convex lens has the ability to disperse light rays. As a consequence, the image formed by such lenses appears to be larger than the real object. Thus, the piece of plastic form the plastic bottles magnifies objects.
